Costs

This section will give insights to entrepreneurs about the several types of costs associated with running a business, both startup costs and ongoing operating costs, which can be divided into fixed and variable costs.

Learning about the cost of business is essential for budgeting, ensuring there is enough capital to cover expenses, and risk management. Cost analysis and strategic pricing based on cost will allow a business to find the optimum price to maximise profitability. Also, the different costs of running a physical store business compared to an online business providing service.

Data for this section will be gathered from primary sources, which will be attained by asking small businesses about their financial structures.
